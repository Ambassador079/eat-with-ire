Place your images in this folder:

REQUIRED:
  logo.png      – Your brand logo (PNG with transparent background, any size, ~200px tall recommended)
  hero-bg.jpg   – Hero section background image (food photo, min 1920x1080px recommended)

MENU IMAGES:
  chicken-chips.jpg   – Chicken & Chips
  ofada-rice.jpg      – Ofada Rice & Plantain Sauce
  noodles.jpg         – Noodles, Egg & Chicken

Then update src/data/menuData.js:
  image: '/images/chicken-chips.jpg'

If no image is set, cards show a clean "Add Photo" placeholder.
